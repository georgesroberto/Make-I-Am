MAKE I AM - PROFILE

TALENT:

Kennedy Gakere commonly and professionally referred to as MAKE I AM alias MIA is a Rapper and Singer, Songwriter, Performing Artist and a Teacher.

MUSIC CAREER:

Make I Am was born and grew up in Central Kenya. He began composing and recording music in 2016. It was after listening to Rapper Khaligraph Jones debut singles one of them being '' Julius Yego'' about the same year. His songs are mostly fused with HipHop, Rap, Trap and Soul music genres. His first two songs ''Hope'' and ''Calling Shots'' were received in an unexpectedly exciting manner even though they were made in a bedroom studio and were never published. This motivated him to save some money and do his first official debut single MONDAY produced by CMG records a studio credited for the rise of self-made stars Deniro and Django from Thika. The project received salutation by the renowned Radio Host Corine Onyango of Homeboyz Radio. It received an airplay on the JUMP OFF show with Corine on Homeboyz radio 103.5FM and this motivated him to record more. In 2017 he performed on his first ever stage at a show hosted by the famous Kwambox. He performed alongside legendary names like, the sensational Naiboi, Kristoff from Busia, Gabu from P-unit, the legendary Chiwawa and the dancehall geek Rankadar. Even with zero experience he still managed to win over the crowd. In 2018, he performed on the same stage as the soft-spoken Gilad (the Swahili Mzungu). Same year he was featured on the show “ikumbi riia wethe” of Inooro Tv from Royal media services and also Mt Kenya Tv. He has since then performed on several other gigs and events among them Sanaa Na Kahawa at Embu, the Bosses Affair of Western Delicacy at Hurlingham, Jamii Executive Gardens at Mwea, Year 2022 Fresher’s Bash at Kirinyaga University where he has shared same stage with renowned and prominent artists as Stephen Mercy and Exray (Taniua). He has recorded over 40 unreleased singles and has recently published two singles and one mixtape distributed by distrokid.com named respectively as REALITY CHECK, NO LOVE and GENESIS, which embodies 5 tracks, available on all digital streaming/ media platforms. He has shot four videos, one directed by Cyro Beats and the later directed by Akili Ya Babu Concepts, alias Tsyder or Toni known from the show Tahidi High. Because of his exponential energy, he has managed to attract a considerably large audience with over 6000 followers on Twitter which is his favorite social media, and over 1000 subscribers on YouTube, as well as followers, fans and friends all over on other social media/ digital streaming platforms.

COMMUNITY ATTACHMENT:

He formed a company by the name TOOLKITT that focuses on bringing all forms of arts together and fosters the cooperation of various artists in enhancing the community. Together and with collaboration of other brands he has done various community projects among them GOTTEA MSEE in December 2019, which was a campaign to raise awareness on the challenges faced by Street Children in Mwea town in Kirinyaga County, which was a major success. ARTISTRY: Make I Am is a powerful Lyricist. He considers himself a protégé of TUPAC SHAKUR, JERMAINE COLE, KENDRICK LAMAR, SHAWN CARTER and DWAYNE CARTER among many other music Legends. His music can be described as entertaining, therapeutic and objective. He can rap and still can swing a soothing melody gloomed in stylist wordplays, but mostly prides himself in his knack to engage the mind of the listener with powerful lyrics that are crafted with precision. He is keen to maintain rhyme and meaning with his writing skills, projecting out life as a young black man growing up in Africa. His music has international potential and when well invested on can generate enormous returns. His role model is BURNA BOY of Nigeria. MAKE I AM is committed to improving his brand and expanding his music abilities as he continues to grace the entertainment arena, show casing his versatility in music. His songs are available in all digital streaming/ media platforms.
